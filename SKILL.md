# TimePortal Auto Evolution Video Generator

This skill generates prompts for cinematic automotive evolution videos.

Video structure:

Phase 0 — bare chassis opening  
Phase 1–10 — vehicle generations  

Total runtime:

75–80 seconds.

Each phase contains:

3 seconds driving  
2 seconds disassembly  
2 seconds assembly

---

# Rules

Camera angle must NEVER change.

Vehicles always move forward.

Driver must always be visible.

Lighting must always be overcast natural daylight.

No morphing effects.

All transformations must be mechanical assembly/disassembly.
